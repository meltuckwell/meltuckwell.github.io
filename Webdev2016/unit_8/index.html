Helping mums feel fit and fabulous - Post Natal Exercise Classes

Whatever your stage of motherhood our fitness classes will boost your well-being and help you feel full of confidence.

Book a class

Buggyfit

Fun, outdoor exercise class for mums with babies in buggies. An hour long power walk, with stops along the way for strength-ening and toning exercises that work the whole body.

Mumacise

One-hour low impact cardio and body conditioning class to help regain your post baby shape and get your mummy confidence back! Baby comes too so no need for childcare. Focus on toning mummy ummies, bottoms, legs and arms as well as strengthening those all important pelvic floors!

Fitmumas

An hour dedicated to mummies who are serious about getting back into shape. Using a combination of cardio interval training, core abdominal work and all over body conditioning exercises, we work hard to get you feeling fitter, slimmer and full of vitality.
